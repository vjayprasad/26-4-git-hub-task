Description
Oy! is a 2009 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film written and directed by debutanat Anand Ranga. The film stars Siddharth and Shamili (in her first leading role),[1] while Sunil and Ali play supporting roles. The music of the film was composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja.[2] The story revolves around Uday (Siddharth) fulfilling the wishes of Sandhya (Shamili) during her last days, as the latter was diagnosed with cancer. The film was released on 3 July 2009. The film is loosely based on the 2002 film A Walk to Remember. The film was dubbed in Tamil as Kadhal Alai.[3] Though the film didn't do well at the box office during its initial release, it gained a strong cult following over the years and has remained as a favorite of many. It has been re-released on valentine's day of 2024, many people collectively praised the film for not just songs but also for the storyline.
