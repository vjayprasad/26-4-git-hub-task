Agnyaathavaasi (lit.â€‰' person in exile') is a 2018 Indian Telugu-language action drama film[3][4] written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas. The film stars Pawan Kalyan, Aadhi Pinisetty, Keerthy Suresh and Anu Emmanuel in lead roles. The film began its principal photography in April 2017 which ended in November 2017. Anirudh Ravichander composed the film's score and soundtrack. Upon release on 10 January 2018, during the Sankranti festival weekend, it received mixed-to-negative reviews from both critics and audiences, alike. The performances of the cast, dialogues, production values, cinematography, songs, background score, and action sequences received praise. However, the direction, writing, screenplay, pace were criticized.[5] The film, released under big expectations, could not manage to make an impact at the box office and was a box office disaster, recovering less than half of its investment.[6] The film was dubbed in Malayalam as Yuvarajavu and later it was dubbed in Hindi as Yevadu 3.
